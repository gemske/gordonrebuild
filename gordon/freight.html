Gordon International Freight Program

Shipping:

Gordon International offers a guaranteed freight program. This program will provide fixed shipping and handling costs and enable the purchaser to determine freight and handling charges at time of order placement. Delivery will be via regional and national freight carriers or Gordon International truck. Shipping and handling are pre-calculated and pre-paid by Gordon International. Delivery and handling charges are preferential and added to the original invoice at time of shipment. How to participate:

  • Determine the zone to which products will be shipped
  • Use the chart below to calculate your shipping / handling charges as a percentage of total COM LIST price
  • Indicate "GI Guaranteed Freight Program" on your Purchase order Gordon International will:
  • Select the carrier
  • Include your pre-calculated freight charges as part of your Order Acknowledgement and Invoice


    Exclusions: All marble and glass tables are excluded from this program and must be quoted on a per order basis. Please contact our sales support team for assistance at 800-446-9872.
